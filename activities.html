Life at School

Where learning meets fun through diverse creative and physical activities.

Our Activities

We offer a diverse range of activities that nurture talent, build character, and encourage holistic development.

Taekwondo & Silambam

Building discipline and defense through Taekwondo (twice weekly) and the ancient Tamil martial art of Silambam, mentored by expert trainers.

Martial Arts Training

Music & Dance

Developing rhythm and expression through choral singing, instruments, and classical folk dances performed at school events.

Sports & Games

"A sound mind in a sound body." We dedicate 45 minutes daily to games and sports, ensuring every child stays active and develops teamwork skills.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Fostering mental peace and physical flexibility through daily yoga sessions and mindfulness exercises.

Academics

Following the TN State Syllabus (English Medium). We specialize in Grammar (English & Tamil), Computer Science, Hindi, Handwriting, and G.K.

Field Trips

Educational excursions to waterfalls, temples, and heritage sites that bring learning to life beyond the classroom walls.

Scouts & NCC

Instilling patriotism, leadership, and discipline through scout training, flag ceremonies, and community service activities.

Harmonizing Mind & Body

Empowering Students Through Yoga

Our specialized yoga program is designed to improve concentration, reduce stress, and promote physical well-being. We believe that a calm mind is the foundation for effective learning.

Daily Practice

15-minute morning sessions for all classes to center focus.

Flexibility & Health

Focus on posture, breathing, and physical stamina.

Cultural Celebrations

We celebrate all major festivals with great enthusiasm, helping children understand our rich cultural heritage and values of togetherness.

Guru Pooja & Annual Day
Traditional Festivals (Navaratri Kolu, Pongal, Diwali)
Pooja Celebrations (Saraswathi, Vinayagar Chathurthi)
