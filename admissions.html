Admissions

Joining the Kamarajar family is easy. Follow the simple steps below to begin your child's journey.

Admission Process

01

Visit & Enquiry

Visit our campus for a tour and meet our admission counselors to understand our curriculum.

02

Registration

Obtain the application form from the school office and submit the filled-out form with required fees.

03

Interaction

An informal interaction with the child and parents to understand the child's readiness.

04

Confirmation

Upon approval, complete the documentation and fee payment to secure the seat.
