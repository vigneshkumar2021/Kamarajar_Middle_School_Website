# Kamarajar Nursery and Middle School Website

Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures with Love, Wisdom, and Service since 1981.

## 🏫 About the School

Founded in 1981 by **Mr. L. Manimaran**, Kamarajar Nursery and Middle School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Muhavoor, Tamil Nadu, for over 45 years. Guided by the principles of K. Kamarajar, our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where children can reach their full potential.

- **Motto**: "Love — Wisdom — Service"
- **Location**: 2,13 Aathi Dravider, Vannar Street, Muhavoor, Tamil Nadu 626111
- **Focus**: Holistic development through academic excellence and co-curricular activities.

## ✨ Core Features

### 📚 Academic Excellence
- **Syllabus**: Following the Tamil Nadu State Syllabus (English Medium).
- **Specialized Subjects**: Grammar (English & Tamil), Computer Science, Hindi, Handwriting, and General Knowledge.
- **Pass Rate**: Proudly maintaining a 100% passing rate with over 8,000 alumni.

### 🥋 Holistic Development
- **Martial Arts**: Regular training in Taekwondo and Silambam (ancient Tamil martial art).
- **Physical Education**: 45 minutes of dedicated daily sports and games.
- **Creative Arts**: Choral singing, instrumental music, and traditional folk dances.
- **Youth Organizations**: Active Bharat Scouts & Guides and NCC (National Cadet Corps) units.
- **Experiential Learning**: Regular field trips to heritage sites, temples, and waterfalls.

### 🏫 Modern Facilities
- **Smart Classrooms**: Bright, clean, and child-friendly learning environments.
- **Safe Campus**: CCTV surveillance, controlled entry/exit, and 24/7 security.
- **Play Area**: Safe, international-standard outdoor playground with rubberized safety flooring.
- **Health**: Dedicated first-aid facilities and trained staff.

### ❤️ Social Responsibility
- **RTE Implementation**: Providing free education to 25% of the student community under the RTE Act 2009 (Std 1 to VIII).

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

The website is a modern, responsive, and visually high-performance static site built with:

- **Structure**: HTML5, Semantic Elements
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (UI/Layout), Bootstrap 5 (Components)
- **Animations**: AOS (Animate On Scroll) for smooth reveal transitions
- **Typography**: [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit) via Google Fonts
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with custom CSS for masonry layouts and interactive hover effects.

## 📂 Project Structure

- `index.html`: Home page with school stats and highlights.
- `about.html`: Detailed history, mission, and leadership messages.
- `admissions.html`: Admission process, eligibility criteria, and required documents.
- `facilities.html`: Showcasing the campus infrastructure and faculty.
- `activities.html`: Highlighting life at school, sports, and cultural events.
- `teacher.html`: Profiles of our dedicated educators and school mentors.
- `gallery.html`: A masonry-style visual journey of school life with video support.
- `contact.html`: Communication channels and interactive map links.
- `images/`: High-quality assets for school events, campus, and logos.
- `teachers/`: Faculty profile images.
- `videos/`: Highlight reels from sports days and celebrations.

## 🚀 Local Development

To run this project locally, simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser. Since it uses CDNs for CSS and JS frameworks, an active internet connection is recommended for the best experience.
