About Our School

Nurturing young minds with care, values, and excellence since 1990.

Our History

Kamarajar Nursery and Middle School was established in 1981 as kindergarten classes with a vision to provide quality education. Founded by Mr. L. Manimaran, B.A(ENG), B.Ed DCE., D.El.Ed., the school carries forward the legacy of K. Kamarajar by empowering every child through learning and discipline.

Over the past 45 years, our institution has grown from a small initiative into a flourishing school upgraded to VIII standard, known for its academic excellence and holistic development of students in Muhavoor.

Our Motto

"Love — Wisdom — Service" — These three pillars guide our educational journey, ensuring every student develops a compassionate heart and a sharp mind.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where children can discover their potential through innovative teaching and holistic activities.

Free Education

Guided by the Central Government order and the **RTE Act 2009**, we are proud to offer free education to 25% of our student community.

RTE Act Implementation

We are committed to social justice and equality in education. This year, we have extended free education benefits to students from I to VIII standard.
