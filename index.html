About Our School

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Founded in 1981 as kindergarten classes, Kamarajar Nursery and Middle School has grown into a premier institution. Under the visionary leadership of our founder
Mr. L. Manimaran B.A ,B.Ed. DCE., D.El.Ed. Correspondent , we have been providing high-quality education and nurturing students for over 45 years.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where children can discover their potential through innovative teaching and holistic activities.

Our Motto

"Love — Wisdom — Service" — Guided by these values, we prepare students to honor truth and justice at all times.
